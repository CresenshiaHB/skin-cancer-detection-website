How to Spot A Skin Cancer?

The ABCDE guide helps you recognize melanoma's warning signs.

  • A
    Asymmetry: One half of the mole does not match the other half.
  • B
    Border: irregular, notched, or blurred.
  • C
    Color: uneven, with multiple shades of brown, black, or even patches of red, white, or blue.
  • D
    Diameter or Dark: larger than 6mm or is noticeably darker than your other moles.
  • E
    Evolving: changes over time in size, shape, color, or develops new symptoms like itching or bleeding.
